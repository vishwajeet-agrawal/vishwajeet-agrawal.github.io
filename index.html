Tap anywhere on screen to get the next colour.

Blindfold yourself and then press SPACE to start the experiment. 500 milliseconds after you press SPACE, a colour will be displayed on the screen. Choose the perceived color by pressing '1' for red, '2' for green and '3' for blue. As you press one of these keys, the response will be recorded and the chosen colour will be displayed for 500 milliseconds, and then the next colour will be displayed. To halt the experiment, press SPACE again.
